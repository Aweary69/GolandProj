Smart Exchange – Trade-In Program by DriveHub

Exchange your car for a new or pre-owned vehicle at a fair market price.

Why Choose Smart Exchange?

  • Convenience: A dedicated manager will assist you throughout the trade-in process.
  • Time-Saving: The dealer immediately credits your car’s value towards a new vehicle.
  • Safety: No need to meet with questionable buyers or handle endless showings.
  • Commission: Sell your pre-owned car on your terms through the dealership.
  • Fast Buyout: Get a competitive evaluation and quick purchase of your vehicle.

How It Works?

  1. Get your car evaluated by our experts.
  2. Receive an offer based on its market value.
  3. Trade it in and choose a new or pre-owned vehicle.
  4. Drive away with your new car hassle-free!

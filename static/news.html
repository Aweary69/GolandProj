Recent Updates

DriveHub Expands Production

DriveHub has announced the opening of new production facilities in Kazakhstan, marking a significant step in strengthening local manufacturing. With these new facilities, the company aims to reduce dependency on imported parts, cut down production costs, and ensure faster delivery times. The expansion is expected to create hundreds of new jobs, boost the regional economy, and enhance the overall availability of DriveHub vehicles. According to the CEO, this move aligns with the company's long-term strategy of sustainable growth and localized production to meet increasing demand.

New Hyundai Model Unveiled

Hyundai has officially unveiled its latest model, setting new benchmarks in the automotive industry with its advanced safety features, fuel efficiency, and sleek design. The new vehicle boasts cutting-edge AI-assisted driving capabilities, a modern infotainment system, and an eco-friendly hybrid engine. Experts predict that this model will compete strongly with other market leaders and redefine the driving experience. Hyundai representatives emphasized their commitment to sustainability, with this launch being a part of their broader initiative to transition towards electric mobility.

DriveHub Partners with Local Dealers

DriveHub is expanding its dealer network by partnering with local automotive dealerships across the country. This initiative aims to improve customer service, provide better access to vehicles, and ensure faster maintenance support. By working closely with local partners, DriveHub intends to make its vehicles more accessible, offering attractive financing options and exclusive dealership benefits. The partnership also includes a training program for sales and service personnel to enhance customer experience and maintain high service standards.

Electric Vehicles Coming Soon

DriveHub has announced its ambitious plans to enter the electric vehicle (EV) market with locally manufactured models. The company aims to introduce a range of EVs that focus on affordability, long battery life, and fast-charging capabilities. According to DriveHub's research team, these upcoming models will cater to both urban and long-distance commuters, making EV adoption easier and more practical. Government incentives for electric vehicles are also expected to play a key role in supporting DriveHub’s strategy, making EVs more accessible to the general public.
